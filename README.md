# Cafe Henna

Welcome to Cafe Henna! Run your own cafe where you do henna art on your customers. Pick a design or do your own custom one. Trace the lines, then click complete. View all of your creations in your library! This game was made in Godot. Music was taken off of Youtube and the art was inspired by Good Pizza Great Pizza.

More henna designs and a shop will be added in the future.

It can be played [here](https://nityadesaraju.itch.io/cafe-henna).
